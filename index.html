Turkhan Mirzayev, Esq

Founder

Born into a family of lawyers and scholars, Mr. Mirzayev has dedicated himself to the legal profession. Not only has he always wanted to become a lawyer, it really was his childhood dream. Known for never giving up on his goals, after gaining over 10 years of experience in immigration law, graduating from New York Law School and passing the New York State Bar Exam, Mr. Mirzaev founded “TM LAW FIRM, PLLC” , the firm that specializes in various immigration matters.

Mr. Mirzayev is a member of the New York State Bar Association, Member of the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA), Member of the American Bar Association, member of the New York City Bar association, Member of Federal Bar Association, member of the National Bar Association and Served as a chairman for the New York office of the European Economic Chamber of Trade Commerce and Industry, LTD. In addition Mr. Mirzayev is admitted to practice law before the Federal courts.
